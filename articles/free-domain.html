How to get a free domain

Heres how to get a free 2nd-level domain.

Why should I get my own Domain with chanchable nameservers?

There a lot of free subdomain providers with non-chanchable nameservers.
For a own article on subdomains, see here. But if you use one of these provides, you cannot chanche their nameservers and you lose a lot of controls and features that are aviable on non-subdomains.
(Note: technicly, what you can get using this tutorial, actually isn't a real domain, but it's on the public suffix list, so you can connect it to your favourite DNS-provider, e.g. Cloudflare.)
